Taylor S. Smith

Writer, Photographer, Minimalist

Born and raised in LA, Taylor ran away to San Francsico when he was 17 and joined the local Art Scene, taking shots and walking the streets.

My uncle once published an article about me in the New York Times off handedly refering to me as an anarchist, nihillist, bohemian. When I was 15.

Three often mistunderstood concepts,

As I've grown older they sort of blended into my subconcious, forming a seperate kind of demon.

Park City, UT

Suffering a nervous breakdown in San Francsico in his mid-twenties, Taylor served time in the kitchens and bars of Main Street during high season picking up various service industy tricks from across the globe
